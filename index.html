Pros and Cons of Public Transportation

I will be talking the Pros and Cons of Public Transportation.

Pros Cons
Cost-effective- Public transportation like buses are usually cheaper than driving a personal car. OverCrowding- There are usually a lot of people taking the same vehicle as you, especially in crowded areas.
Traffic reduction- Helps alleviate traffic congestion by taking cars off the road. Safety Concerns - Your in a car with many different people and you may never if something bad might happen like a robbery.
Convenience for certain people - Can be a good option for people who live near a train station or bus stop. Litmited flexibility - Schedules may not always conveniently align with individuals needs or destination.
